About this site

Hi! My name is Will and I'm a junior in the University of Michigan's School of Information. This scalable & responsive website is my final project for web design and, since I already have a portfolio, I figured that I would make a site that acted as a show-case for a few of my more artistically inclined friends. On this site you can see samples of the work of Ben Swanson-Ralph, Brielle Bonetti, and Michie Nimsombun- all very talented in their respective concentrations. For more information about them, visit their about-pages. To see their work, check out their galleries. Enjoy the site!
